Scholarship Opportunities

Explore scholarships available in Ghana and around the world

Ghana Scholarship Secretariat (Local & Foreign Scholarships)

Provides scholarships for Ghanaian students to study in Ghana or abroad. Applications usually open in March/April.

How to Apply: Visit www.scholarshipgh.com and create an account to start your application.

Mastercard Foundation Scholarship at KNUST

Full scholarships for brilliant but needy students to study at Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology.

How to Apply: Visit mcf.knust.edu.gh to download the form and follow the application guide.

DAAD Scholarship (Germany)

Scholarships for graduates and professionals to pursue Master’s and PhD degrees in Germany.

How to Apply: Visit www.daad.de/en/ and filter scholarships by field and country.

Commonwealth Shared Scholarship

Offers scholarships to students from developing Commonwealth countries to pursue Master’s studies in the UK.

How to Apply: Go to cscuk.fcdo.gov.uk and check eligibility and deadlines.
