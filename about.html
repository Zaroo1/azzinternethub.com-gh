About AZZ Internet Hub

Who We Are

Founded in 2023 by Abdul-Aziz Zaroo, AZZ Internet Hub began as a small online internet cafe with a vision to bridge the digital divide in Ghana. It is the first fully online internet cafe in Ghana and if not in the world

Today, we've grown into a comprehensive digital services provider, helping thousands of students and professionals navigate the digital world.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and professional digital solutions that empower individuals and businesses to thrive in the digital age.
