Frequently Asked Questions

How do I check university admission requirements?

Our University Guides section provides detailed admission requirements for all major Ghanaian universities. You can find specific program cut-off points, subject requirements, and application procedures for each institution.

What digital skills are most valuable for students?

We recommend starting with basic computer literacy, then progressing to:

  1. Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
  2. Basic web development (HTML/CSS)
  3. Graphic design (Canva, Photoshop basics)
  4. Digital marketing fundamentals
  5. Data analysis skills

Our Digital Skills courses start from just 130 GHS.

How can I improve my WASSCE/SSCE results?

Effective strategies include:

  • Solving past questions (available in our Education Resources)
  • Creating a study timetable and sticking to it
  • Joining study groups
  • Seeking help from teachers or tutors for difficult topics
  • Practicing time management during exams
Where can I find scholarships for university?

We maintain an updated database of scholarships in our Financial Aid section. Key sources include:

  • Government scholarships (GETFund)
  • University-specific scholarships
  • Corporate scholarships (MTN, Vodafone, etc.)
  • International opportunities

Check back regularly as new opportunities are added frequently.

How do I prepare for university interviews?

University interview preparation tips:

  1. Research the program and university thoroughly
  2. Prepare to discuss why you chose the program
  3. Review your academic background
  4. Practice common interview questions
  5. Dress appropriately and arrive early
  6. Prepare questions to ask the interviewers

Our Career Preparation resources include mock interview questions.
