Admission Requirements

Key Admission Categories:

Important Notes

  • Cut-off points are for the 2024/2025 academic year and may change annually
  • 1st Choice cut-off points apply to applicants who placed the program as their first choice
  • Full Fee Paying options have different cut-off points and fee structures
  • Some programs have specific subject requirements beyond the aggregate score
  • Distance Education programs have higher cut-off points than regular programs
  • Always verify requirements with the official University of Ghana website before applying
