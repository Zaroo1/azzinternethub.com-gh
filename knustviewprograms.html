Complete KNUST Programs and Cut-Off Points (2024/2025)

Admission Requirements

Minimum Entry Requirements: Aggregate 24 or better (WASSCE/SSSCE)

Core Subjects: Credit passes (A1-C6) in six (6) subjects including:

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Three (3) relevant elective subjects

Note: Cut-off aggregates vary annually based on program demand and available spaces.
